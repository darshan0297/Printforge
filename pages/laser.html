📋 File prep tips
✅Use SVG or DXF for cut files — they give exact dimensions and clean paths.
✅Set your document size to the actual dimensions of the finished part.
✅Use red (RGB 255,0,0) for cut lines, black for engraving — this is standard LightBurn convention.
✅Minimum feature size: 1mm for cuts, 0.5mm for engrave lines.
⚠️Don't nest cut and engrave paths in the same colour — keep them on separate layers.
💡Not sure? Just send a PNG or PDF with your design idea and we'll handle the rest.
