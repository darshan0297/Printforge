About PrintForge

We make impossible prints possible.

PrintForge is a workshop-direct 3D printing studio based in Mount Lavinia, Sri Lanka. We specialise in high-detail cosplay props, functional prototypes, and custom-engineered parts — all printed and finished in-house.

Every order is handled personally. No outsourcing. No middlemen. You work directly with the person running the printer, which means faster turnaround, better quality control, and real answers to your questions.

We run a fleet of FDM printers including the Elegoo Centauri Carbon and Ender 5 Pro, with materials ranging from standard PLA+ and PETG to TPU, ABS, ASA, and carbon-fill. Our laser cutter handles EVA and acrylic for hybrid prop builds.
